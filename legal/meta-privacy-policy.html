Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Facebook Lead Form

Effective Date: January 2025

At Vinci Properties, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share the personal information you provide through our Facebook lead form. It also outlines your rights regarding your information and how to exercise them.

Information We Collect

When you submit a lead form, we may collect the following information:
Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and any additional details you choose to provide.
Property Information: Details about the property you are interested in selling.
Other Voluntarily Provided Information: Any other details you share with us.
We do not collect sensitive personal information unless explicitly provided by you.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  1. Contact you about the property you are interested in selling.
  2. Evaluate your property and provide relevant offers or services.
  3. Deliver personalized support and communication regarding our services.
    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share your information with:
    Service Providers and Partners: Trusted third parties who assist us in delivering our services, under strict confidentiality agreements.
    Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights or comply with legal obligations.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your personal information, including:
Data Encryption: Secure storage of data using encryption technologies.
Access Controls: Restricting access to personal information to authorized personnel only.
Regular Audits: Periodic reviews of our security practices to ensure ongoing compliance.
While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information, subject to applicable laws:
Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
Correction: Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
Deletion: Request deletion of your data, where permitted by law.
Data Portability: Receive your information in a structured, commonly used format.
Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent for communication or data processing at any time.
To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@propertydeals.info or +1 414-403-9077.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or comply with legal obligations. Once the retention period expires, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Opting Out of Communication

If you wish to opt out of receiving communication from Vinci Properties, you can:
Email us at optout@propertydeals.info.
Follow the "unsubscribe" instructions provided in our emails or text messages.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your experience and gather insights about how you interact with our website or Facebook lead form. For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.
